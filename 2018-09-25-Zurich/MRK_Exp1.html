Load the data from experiment 1 and remove outliers (response time must be between 300 and 3000 ms.) Select the columns of interest. Convert the two-level factors to the [-1, 1] encoding as that is more stable than [-0.5, 0.5] when forming high-order interactions.

julia> d2 = @linq load("MRK_Exp1.rds") |>
           where(300 .≤ :rt .≤ 3000) |>
           select(:rrt, :P, :F, :Q, :L, :W, :id, :st) |>
           transform(P = 2 .* :P, F = 2 .* :F, Q = 2 .* :Q, L = 2 .* :L, W = 2 .* :W)
